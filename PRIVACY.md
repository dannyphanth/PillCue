PillCue collects your email address to create and secure your account using Firebase Authentication.
Your medication information (name, dosage, schedule) is stored in Google Firebase Firestore and linked to your account.
When you scan a prescription bottle, the image is sent to Google’s cloud services (Firebase Functions and Gemini API) only to extract text and structured medication details. We do not show ads, sell your data, or share it with advertisers.
You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting <your email>.
We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
