PillCue Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

PillCue ("we", "our", or "us") collects your email address to create and secure your account using Firebase Authentication.

Your medication information (name, dosage, schedule) is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore and linked to your account. This data is only accessible to you when signed in.

When you scan a prescription bottle, the image is sent securely to Google's cloud services (Firebase Cloud Functions and Gemini API) only to extract medication details. Images are not stored after processing and are not used for any other purpose.

We do not show ads, sell your data, or share it with advertisers or third parties for marketing purposes.

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting dannyphanth@gmail.com.

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
